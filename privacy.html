Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how That AI Guy ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the That AI Guy iOS application.

1. Information we collect

Audio data

That AI Guy uses your iPhone's microphone to listen to conversations. Audio is captured in short segments and transcribed entirely on your device using Apple's built-in speech recognition (SFSpeechRecognizer). Audio is never transmitted anywhere — only the resulting text transcript is sent for analysis.

Transcribed text

Short text transcripts (maximum 600 characters per segment) are sent directly from your device to your chosen AI provider — either Anthropic or Google — using your own API key. We have no backend server and never receive, see, or store any transcript data.

API key

You supply your own AI provider API key during setup. It is stored exclusively in iOS Secure Storage on your device and is never transmitted to us. It is sent only to your chosen provider (Anthropic or Google) as part of direct API calls made by the app.

Names and keywords (Roasting feature)

The Roasting feature allows you to enter a person's name and optional descriptive keywords. This information is sent to your chosen AI provider for that session only. It is not retained by us, logged, or stored beyond the API call.

Usage data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics (such as session length and feature interactions) to improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify you.

Device information

We may collect basic device information (such as iOS version and device model) for debugging and compatibility purposes.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate real-time corrections, observations, and responses based on detected speech
  • To improve the accuracy and quality of the app
  • To diagnose technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-party services

That AI Guy sends transcribed text to one of the following AI providers, depending on your choice at setup:

  • Anthropic (Claude Haiku) — anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google (Gemini Flash) — policies.google.com/privacy

You select your provider and supply your own API key. Data sent to these providers is governed by their respective privacy policies. We do not act as an intermediary — API calls are made directly from your device to the provider.

4. Data retention

We operate no backend servers and retain no user data. Transcripts are processed in real time on your device and sent directly to your AI provider — they are not stored by us at any point. Your API key remains on your device in iOS Secure Storage until you delete it.

Anonymised usage analytics may be retained for up to 12 months.

5. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

You can revoke microphone access at any time via Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone on your iPhone. You can delete all locally stored data (including your API key) using Delete All Data in the app's Settings.

6. Data security

All API calls are made directly from your device to your chosen provider over HTTPS. Your API key is stored in iOS Secure Storage (encrypted, sandboxed to this app). We have no servers that could be breached to expose your data.

7. Children's privacy

That AI Guy is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
