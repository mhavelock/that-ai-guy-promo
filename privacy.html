Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how That AI Guy ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the That AI Guy iOS application.

1. Information we collect

Audio data

That AI Guy uses your iPhone's microphone to listen to conversations in the room. Short audio segments are captured, transcribed, and sent to a third-party language model to generate responses. We do not permanently store audio recordings on our servers.

Usage data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics (such as session length and feature interactions) to improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify you.

Device information

We may collect basic device information (such as iOS version and device model) for debugging and compatibility purposes.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate real-time comedic responses based on detected speech
  • To improve the accuracy and quality of the app
  • To diagnose technical issues

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-party services

That AI Guy uses a third-party large language model (LLM) API to process transcribed audio and generate responses. Transcribed text may be transmitted to this service. Their use of that data is governed by their own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any third-party services we use.

4. Data retention

Audio recordings are processed in real time and are not retained on our servers beyond the duration required to generate a response. Anonymised usage analytics may be retained for up to 12 months.

5. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

You can revoke microphone access at any time via Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone on your iPhone.

6. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. All data transmissions use HTTPS encryption.

7. Children's privacy

That AI Guy is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
